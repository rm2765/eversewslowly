Behind the Seams

Last year, I joined the BKLYN Fashion Academy’s sixth season—an incredible program that more aspiring designers should know about. The Academy (BFA) takes designers on a four-month journey to a runway finale. The 2024 cohort created mini-collections under the Women in Future Industries theme. I interviewed fellow designers Gia, Regina, and Ronen, and together, we tell it all—from the creative challenges that tested both our skills and resolve, to our growth as design professionals.
